강의개요 및 목적

This course provides students with an understanding of how animals adapt physiologically to environmental challenge and addressed the basic physical and chemical principles that underlie physiological processes. Several biological systems are considered including transport through plasma membrane, signal transduction, nervous, endocrine, and muscle.

교재 및 참고문헌
